Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AITA Stories Bot ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize the App via TikTok, we may collect:

  • Your TikTok User ID (open_id) and access token — used solely to post videos on your behalf
  • Basic TikTok profile information required by the TikTok API

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any personally identifiable information beyond what TikTok's API provides during authorization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
  • Post automated video content to your TikTok account as authorized
  • Maintain the functionality of the App

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and user IDs are stored locally on the server running the App. We use industry-standard security practices to protect this data. Tokens are never shared with unauthorized parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your access token and user ID only as long as you continue to use the App. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or revoking App access in your TikTok settings.

5. TikTok API Data

This App uses the TikTok API. Any data received from TikTok is used solely for the purpose of posting content and is handled in accordance with TikTok's Privacy Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses Reddit's public API to source content. No personal user data is sent to Reddit. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok Settings → Security → Authorized Apps

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
